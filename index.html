About the Event

On Sunday, 19 July 2026, Art of Living Guru Puja Pandits will travel across Chembur, Tilaknagar, Ghatkopar,Sion and BARC to conduct Guru Pujas in the homes. Slots run hourly from 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Let us know your preferred time below, and our team will confirm your booking and assign a Pandit.

Instructions for Devotees

  1. Please provide a correct address and mobile number. The assigned Guru Puja Pandit will call you on the mobile number provided.
  2. You will receive the Puja confirmation and the details of the Guru Puja Pandit assigned by email.
  3. Invite your near and dear ones in your building for the Puja.
  4. The Guru Puja Pandit assigned will send you the list of items to be arranged for the Puja.
